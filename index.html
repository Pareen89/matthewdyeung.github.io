Hello!

My name is Matthew Yeung, I am currently a research affiliate at Lawrence Berkeley National Labs working on a thin-film growth of Metal-Organic Chalcogenide Assemblies and on a proposal for Ultra-Fast Optically Modulated Field Emission Devices.

I have applied to Ph.D. programs for the next fall and plan to pursue my Ph.D. in either materials science, electrical engineering, or applied physics. I am particularly interested in research that is at the intersection of materials science and electrical engineering. Last update (December 15, 2017)
